Marwen Belkaid

Researcher - Computational modeling, Neuroscience, Robotics

Welcome to my Webpage!

I am a researcher in Neuroscience and Robotics. My work aims to model cognitive processes for the purpose of analyzing and better understanding the brain, but also of drawing inspiration to develop intelligent machines. I am particularly interested in decision-making, emotion, and social interactions. I obtained my doctorate at the Université de Cergy-Pontoise in France in December 2016. Then, from 2017 to 2022, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Sorbonne Université, in Paris, and then at the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia in Genoa, Italy. Since September 2022, I hold a Junior Professor Chair in Neuroscience and Robotics at CY Cergy Paris Université where I develop my research at the ETIS Lab.
